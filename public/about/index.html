About Islamic Light

Islamic Light is a bilingual Islamic website built to provide beneficial resources in Arabic and English. The platform includes Quran reading, prayer time tools, articles, daily reminders, and moderated community content.

Our goal is to make useful Islamic knowledge easier to access through a clear and mobile-friendly experience while keeping public policy and contact information available to visitors.
